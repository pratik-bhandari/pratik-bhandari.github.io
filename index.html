Pratik Bhandari

Hello there! Welcome to my site.

I am a doctoral researcher at the Collaborative Research Center (Sonderforschungsbereichs – SFB 1102) at Saarland University, Department of Psychology and Department of Language Science and Technology. Prof. Jutta Kray and Prof. Vera Demberg are my advisors.

My PhD research is focused on how we make sense of spoken language under adverse listening conditions. I study when (and how) context helps us understand degraded speech, and when context is not much helpful.

I expect to complete my PhD by the end of September this year. Offers for postdoc positions are welcome :)
